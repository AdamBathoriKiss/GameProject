Welcome to Cat VS Aliens!

In this game you can save the world from Aliens with ninja Cat! It will be not easy, but we beleive in you! Use for the UP and DOWN keys for moving the cat and shoot the aliens with pressing SPACE. If one of the aliens go trough on cat's line, then you lost the game. If you can destroy all of the aliens, that means, you are the new hero! Enjoy the game!
